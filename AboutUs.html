Anglo Club is one of the students' club at JNTUK University College of Engineering Narasaraopet dedicated to assisting students in their learning and development of the essential skills they need to succeed in their workplace. Some of the skills include communication skills as they are ability to effectively convey your thoughts and ideas to others, both verbally and in writing. Interpersonal skills are the ability to interact with others in a positive and productive way. Resume building is the process of creating a document that highlights your skills and experience in order to make a good impression on potential employers.

We believe that these skills are critical for success in any career, and we are committed in providing our members with the resources and support they need to thrive. We offer a wide range of activities and resources to our members like,

  • Workshops and seminars on topics such as public speaking, active listening, conflict resolution, resume writing, and interview skills.
  • We host regular social activities and enhance networking opportunities to help our members connect with each other.
  • We offer our members the opportunity to participate in mock interviews. This can help our members feel more confident and prepared when they go on real interviews.
  • Materials are provided and discussion groups on topics related to communication, interpersonal skills, and career development are performed.
  • Job search assistance, including help with writing resumes and cover letters, preparing for interviews.

Our club is open to students from all branches. We are committed to creating a supportive and inclusive environment where everyone can learn and grow. If you are interested in developing the skills you need to succeed in the workplace, we encourage you to join the Anglo Club.
