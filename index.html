About Us

This is one of the student's club at JNTUK University College of Engineering Narasaraopet.
This club focuses on improving your Soft skills and personality development skills as they are essential because it improves our employability and enable students to work harmoniously in dynamic environments.
This includes Leadership, Interpersonal skills, Adaptability, Teamwork, Problem Solving, Time Management, Communication skills and many more...

Our Focuses

Communication Skills

Different aspects of communication skills and how to develope them, group discussion, public speaking, presentation skills and effective listening.

Vocabulary

Having a good command of the Vocabularyof the language will help you to convey your ideas more effectively. It develops your skills in resume building, interviews, fluency, and other writing aactivities.

Interpersonal Skills

Importance of body language, grooming, Interview Skills- How to present oneself during interviews and ways to handle them.

Resume building

Details on structureand types of resumes, difference between CV and Resume, how tyo choose the right format for a resume

Command & Creativity

Having a command of the language gives you a clear vision of how to express yourself which takes you one step closer to your goals and give your creativity a voice on this platform.

Curious Cognition

Anglo Club is a way where individuals come together to engage their curiosity, deepen their understanding and thinking about the language.
